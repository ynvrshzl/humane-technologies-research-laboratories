# Humane Ethical Technology Research Laboratories
this project is twofold, to provide documentation on what exactly is ethical humane technology, as well as group our planned future computer programs and software, for humane ethical technologies. the research laboratory part of the name, comes from the experimental nature of software. it is safe to say, the perfect computer program does not exist. we believe every program should be a balance between engineering and the individual human needs. at the forefront of this emerging field, is ui/ux, because the graphical interface is the first thing the human seas, it is the main method of interaction with a computer system, but it is paradoxically, the last thing the engineer focuses on. designing user interfaces, is an arduous task which combines many creative and scientific fields. perhaps the process should be decoupled from the engineering. the engineer should focus on the ethical programming, and the designer should focus on the experience of interacting with the program. both are very complex tasks. highly experimental in nature. if it is why the only sensible way to approach this, is within our native language, science. researching. hypotheses. experiment! till we finally find that eureka!
